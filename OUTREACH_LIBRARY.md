# ADMF — Outreach Library

Last updated: 2026-06-17

---

## Principles

- Relationship-first. Never cold-pitch.
- Warm, short, professional.
- Reconnect — do not sell.
- No hard selling. No pressure. No false urgency.
- ADMF presents as a long-term design partner, not a freelancer looking for gigs.

---

## Who to Reach Out To

1. Former clients
2. Former colleagues
3. Agency contacts
4. Founders in network
5. Scale-up teams without senior design leadership

---

## Message Structure

Every outreach message should have:

1. **Personal connection** — reference the relationship, shared history, or how you know them
2. **What's new** — brief mention of current focus (senior design + AI workflows)
3. **Soft opening** — leave space for them to respond without pressure
4. **No ask** — or a very soft one if appropriate

Length: short. 3–5 sentences maximum for initial contact.

```
Hi [Name],

If you're ever looking for senior design support, I'd love to reconnect.

Andreas
```

---

## Follow-Up Rule

- One follow-up only, if no response after 2 weeks.
- Never more than two touches on a cold reconnect.
- If they respond but aren't ready: note it, revisit in 3–6 months.

---

## What Not to Do

- Do not attach the PDF unsolicited in the first message
- Do not list all services
- Do not mention pricing
- Do not use "I wanted to reach out" (weak opener)
- Do not use "I hope this finds you well" (generic)
- Do not write more than 5 sentences in an initial message
