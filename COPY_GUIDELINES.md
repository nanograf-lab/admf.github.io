## Positioning Language

**Use:**
- "senior design partner"
- "senior multidisciplinary design partner"
- "direct senior access"
- "without the overhead of a traditional agency"
- "lean partnership"
- "clarity, speed and quality"
- "visual craft and product thinking"

**Avoid:**
- "creative agency"
- "freelancer"
- "studio"
- "revolutionary"
- "transformative"
- "world-class"
- "cutting-edge"
- "we're passionate about"
- Any language that sounds like a startup pitch or agency brochure

---

## Tone Rules

- Write like a senior person talking to another senior person
- Short sentences preferred
- No filler words
- No false urgency
- Confident without being arrogant
- Warm without being sales-y
- Direct without being cold
