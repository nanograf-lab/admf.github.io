## What ADMF Is

ADMF is a senior design partnership.

Not an agency.
Not a freelancer.
Not a studio with layers.

**Positioning:**
A senior multidisciplinary design partner for companies that value clarity, speed and quality — without the overhead of a traditional agency.

## What ADMF Is Not

- A cheap freelance option
- A large agency with account managers and layers
- A full-service branding house
- A traditional advertising agency
- A generalist production shop

## Delivery Model

ADMF is not a traditional agency and should not be treated as a solo freelance practice.

The model is a senior design partnership with direct senior involvement. Depending on the project, additional trusted senior collaborators may participate behind the scenes.

The public positioning emphasizes quality, seniority and direct access rather than agency structures, team size or headcount.

Clients work directly with senior practitioners while ADMF retains the flexibility to assemble the right expertise when appropriate.

- Simplicity beats complexity
- Clarity beats decoration
- Senior judgment beats process theatre
- AI should enhance quality, not replace thinking
- Direct collaboration beats agency layers

## Outreach Strategy

Relationship-first. Not cold pitching.

**Tone:** Warm, short, professional.

**Goal:** Reconnect with former clients, colleagues, agency contacts, founders.

**Approach:**
- Mention portfolio update
- Mention current focus: AI + design
- Mention availability
- No hard selling

**ADMF should always present as:** a long-term design partner, not a freelancer looking for gigs.
